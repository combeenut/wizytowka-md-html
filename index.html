Why MyDarlink?

Advantages you’re getting:

Play videos and read articles at the same time

Here you can watch your favourite videos and read interesting content without switching the tab.

Setup your privacy

MDL gives you many options to set your privacy in the public space. You get to decide who can see your links and how visible you are.

Publish your links

You can make your links available to others with just one click.

Comment and discuss links

MDL provides a simple way to chat, making it easy for you to discuss with others directly in the links’ window.

Delta

Hub between PRIVATE and STREAM. Place where you create your public profile.

Private

Your private space, where you can store your own links.

Stream

Storage of all links published by MyDarlink users.

Categories

Categories which you set in your PRIVATE space or which we proposed in STREAM.

Why beta version?

MyDarlink has just arrived to the Internet world. To provide reliable and free tool we still need to analyse our codes within the closed group of confidential users. Join us and participate in the process of creating the most satisfying tool.

Responsive Design

MyDarlink is available on all possible devices. You get the application that recognizes your device and provides you the most ergonomic pattern for your screen.

Share

Share you links within MyDarlink users and on others by email or on social platforms – Facebook, Twitter, Google +.

Multilingual

Currently MyDarlink is published as Beta version and is only available in English. It will be multilingual to let you set the most convenient language.

Personalize your profile

Set your privacy, create your own public profile and modify the look of our interface.

Analytic tools

We constantly develop analytic algorithms tracing move on MyDarlink profile to display links, in order that matches your interests.

Intelligent ads.

We want to ensure our users, that they won’t be disturbed by unwanted ads using MyDarlink . Section “for me” is for adverts selected especially for you.
